# Digital (Re)Search

A short how to (re)search for and access relevant information in the age of semantic search and open information.

## Useful Search Engines

- <https://kagi.com/>: Ad-free search engine - much better than Google in quality (free tier available)
- <https://exa.ai/search>: Semantic search engine which often spits out relevant papers and blog articles
- <https://www.alphaxiv.org/>: RAG for for [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/) papers
- <https://arxivxplorer.com/>: Semantic search for [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/) papers

## Free Information Resources

- <https://paperpanda.app/search>: SciHub for free access to papers
- <https://www.pdfdrive.com/>: Free access to eBooks
- <https://freedium-mirror.cfd/>: Unlock medium articles
- <https://archive.org/>: Archive of the Web
  - <https://web.archive.org/>: Wayback machine (stores snapshots of websites)
- <https://annas-archive.org/>: Archive of humanity's knowledge
